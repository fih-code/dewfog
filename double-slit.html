the double slit

wave · particle · observer
not watched — a wave pattern is building up
Fire a single particle at two slits, and it lands like a wave that went through both at once — building up a striped pattern over time. But detect which slit it actually uses — with any physical measurement, no one needs to be watching — and that pattern disappears, leaving two plain bands instead. It's the physical interaction that changes the result, not anyone's attention.
